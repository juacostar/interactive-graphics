The main objective of the website is to show a little bit about German culture, more specifically about the city of Munich. It is a proposal in which you will find different aspects of the city of munich as its location, its population, most iconic places to visit, history and an interactive map in which you will find even more wonderful places that make munich one of the best places to visit in Germany and even Europe.
We hope you can get to know and visit through this website some of the most representative places of Munich and also get to learn a little bit of its culture and history, which make this city a dream place to visit and even live. Come and visit Munich, you won't regret it!
